This app is playable on both windows and android.
On android you can either swipe ON THE BOAT, or tap the side of the screen you wish to move towards.
On windows you can also use the right and left arrow keys.
The goal is to collect people and avoid crashing into the debris.
Click on the robot (captain ishka) and he will display a message. 
Click play to start the game
Click highscores to see your personal highscores.
Click how to play to see how to play.
The source for the font was mentioned in the Game Doc. We created the music.
Enjoy...
